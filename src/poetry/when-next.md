Stand at a coffee bar

Get in a car

Hold doors ajar

Not stand so far

Pet a fat pug

Cut a damn rug

Get stung by a bug

Hold a long hug

Chase a bus, miss

Touch something, “this”

Travel deep into abyss

Come up for air—gasp

from a summer long kiss
